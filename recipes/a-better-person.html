Recipe to become a better person

Out of all the recipes, you are my favorite

Life sometimes feels like a messed up recipe, a recipe that has turned out to be a disaster. Everyone's recipe of life is different. Sometimes you are soaring higher than clouds and sometimes you feel so low that you loose the courage to keep fighting. We all have been through shit, we all know that life is not always easy but what we all should know is that giving up and letting go is never the answer. Lets make a recipe, a recipe that will make you a better version of yourself!

Things required:

  • An open mind
  • And the willingness to change

Steps:

You may feel like i am undermining you and assuming that you need to become a better person, as if you are not already a better person. If you think that you already are someone that the 9 year old you would adore then congratulations, nothing wrong with sticking along ey?

  1. Identify your weaknesses
  2. Learn to control your impulses and keep a calm mind
  3. Accept that the only thing you can control are your actions
  4. Do not let other peoples choices affect you
  5. Always love yourself but never be full of yourself

There, You are already a better person!
